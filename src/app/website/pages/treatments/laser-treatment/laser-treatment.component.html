Diode Laser – Permanent Laser Hair Reduction

The diode laser is a highly effective laser system used for permanent hair reduction. It targets the melanin in hair follicles, heating and destroying them to reduce future hair growth. Diode lasers are especially suitable for Indian and darker skin types due to their longer wavelength, which penetrates deeper and minimizes skin damage.

Common treatment areas:
  • Face (upper lip, chin, sideburns)
  • Underarms
  • Arms and legs
  • Bikini and Brazilian areas
  • Chest and back
Benefits of diode laser hair reduction:
  • Safe for all skin types
  • Long-term hair reduction
  • Quick sessions with minimal discomfort
  • Reduces ingrown hairs and folliculitis
  • Improves skin texture over time
What to expect:
  • Multiple sessions (typically 6–8) are needed for optimal results
  • Mild redness or warmth post-procedure; usually subsides within hours
  • No downtime; you can resume normal activities the same day
  • Maintenance sessions may be required annually

Dr. Rohal customizes settings based on your skin tone, hair type, and sensitivity to ensure safe and effective treatment.

CO₂ Laser – Precision Skin Resurfacing & Lesion Removal

The fractional CO₂ laser is a gold-standard treatment for skin resurfacing, scar remodeling, and lesion removal. It works by delivering targeted beams of carbon dioxide laser energy into the skin, creating controlled micro-injuries that stimulate collagen remodeling and skin renewal.

Indications include:
  • Acne scars and post-surgical scars
  • Warts and skin tags
  • Benign moles and seborrheic keratoses
  • Enlarged pores and fine lines
  • Skin resurfacing and rejuvenation
Benefits of CO₂ laser therapy:
  • High precision with minimal damage to surrounding tissue
  • Effective for both superficial and deep skin concerns
  • Collagen stimulation leads to firmer, smoother skin
  • Permanent removal of benign lesions like warts and moles
  • Long-lasting improvements after a few sessions
