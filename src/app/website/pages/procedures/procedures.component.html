PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) Therapy

PRP therapy uses your own blood to enhance skin rejuvenation, reduce hair loss, and improve the overall appearance of your skin. Dr. Chandrakar carefully injects platelet-rich plasma into targeted areas to stimulate collagen production and accelerate healing. How It Works: Blood is drawn from the patient, processed to concentrate platelets, and then re-injected into the skin to promote healing and rejuvenation. Benefits: Improves skin texture, reduces wrinkles, and stimulates hair growth for those with hair loss.

Scar Revision

Scar revision is designed to improve the appearance of scars caused by acne, surgery, or injury. Dr. Chandrakar uses a variety of techniques to minimize scar visibility and promote smoother skin. How It Works: Depending on the type and location of the scar, Dr. Chandrakar may use laser therapy, microdermabrasion, or even surgical excision to reduce scar tissue. Benefits: Softens the appearance of scars, improves skin texture, and promotes healing.

Microdermabrasion

Microdermabrasion is a non-invasive procedure that exfoliates the outer layer of dead skin cells to reveal smoother, healthier skin. It is an effective treatment for acne scars, age spots, and fine lines. How It Works: Tiny crystals are sprayed on the skin to gently exfoliate, followed by suction to remove the exfoliated cells. Benefits: Smoother skin, improved texture, and enhanced skin tone with minimal downtime.

Xanthelasma Removal

Xanthelasma refers to the yellowish fatty deposits that appear on or around the eyelids. Dr. Chandrakar uses advanced methods such as laser treatment or excision to remove these deposits safely. How It Works: Laser or minor surgical procedures are used to precisely remove the xanthelasma, restoring a clearer, smoother eyelid area. Benefits: Removes unsightly yellowish spots, providing a more aesthetically pleasing eyelid appearance.

Skin Biopsy

A skin biopsy is a diagnostic procedure used to examine suspicious moles, growths, or skin conditions. Dr. Chandrakar removes a small sample of skin tissue to test for conditions like skin cancer or other dermatological concerns.

How It Works: A small portion of the skin is removed under local anesthesia and sent to a laboratory for testing.

Benefits: Accurate diagnosis of skin abnormalities, providing valuable information for treatment.

Mole & Cyst Removal

Moles and cysts can be bothersome or even a cause for concern. Dr. Chandrakar offers safe removal techniques, including laser and surgical excision, to remove these growths with minimal scarring.

How It Works: Moles and cysts are excised through minor surgical procedures, ensuring minimal disruption to the surrounding skin.

Benefits: Removes unwanted growths, improving skin appearance and reducing potential health risks.

Botox Injections

Botox is a popular injectable treatment that temporarily relaxes facial muscles, smoothing out wrinkles and fine lines, especially around the forehead, eyes, and mouth.

How It Works: Botox injections block nerve signals, preventing muscles from contracting, which helps reduce the appearance of wrinkles.

Benefits: Non-invasive, quick results, and minimal recovery time, providing a refreshed and youthful appearance.

Thread Lift for Skin Tightening

Thread lifts are a non-surgical procedure designed to lift and tighten sagging skin on the face and neck. Using special threads, Dr. Chandrakar can lift and support the skin for a more youthful contour.

How It Works: Fine threads are inserted under the skin, which are then pulled to lift and tighten the skin. The threads also stimulate collagen production, providing a natural, long-lasting result.

Benefits: Non-surgical, minimal downtime, and effective for lifting sagging skin without the need for facelifts.

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) Skin Tightening

HIFU is a cutting-edge, non-invasive treatment that uses focused ultrasound energy to stimulate deep layers of the skin, promoting collagen production and tightening loose or sagging skin.

How It Works: High-frequency sound waves are directed at specific layers of the skin to stimulate collagen production and tighten the skin.

Benefits: Non-invasive, no downtime, and results in firmer, more youthful-looking skin over time.
